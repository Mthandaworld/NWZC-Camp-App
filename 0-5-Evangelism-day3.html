LESSON THREE – CHILD PREACHERS

Welcome

·         Greet the children and ensure that they are well seated and quite

·         Introduce yourself and tell the children the lesson you are going to be teaching.

·         Start with the theme song of the camp, “I shall not be moved” and have a prayer.

·         Our slogan for Evangelism lessons is as follows:

-          Leader says “Evangelism”, “Evangeliving”, “I am a messenger for Jesus”, “You are a messenger for Jesus”

Explain to the children that:

·         Evangelism means telling others about Jesus.

·         Evangeliving means letting the way you live preach about Jesus. (e.g. the way you talk, the way you dress, what you eat, what you watch, what you listen to and the games you play.

Repeat Campmeeting 2024 Theme

-          Leader says “Campmeeting 2024”, “I shall not be moved. Faithful till the end.”. Sing the song “I shall not be moved”, with the children.

 

Know the names of the children in the class, if the class is small.

Recap of Previous Lesson, key highlights:

Memory Verse

1 Timothy 4:12 New International Version “Don’t let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity.”

 

Lesson Objectives

1.      There is no one who is too young to speak to others about Jesus and His love towards us.

2.      If we love Jesus, we will gladly tell others about Him

 

CONTENT

Everyone, including children, is an ambassador for Jesus.

2 Corinthians 5:20 New King James Version “Now then, we are ambassadors for Christ, as though God were pleading through us” 

What is an ambassador? Explain what an ambassador is.

Ambassador:

An ambassador is an important official selected to represent his/her country in another country. They represent their country in all matters reflecting their countries mannerisms, dressing, language, culture, traditions, food and many more.

We represent heaven here on earth. Wherever you stay, or learn, you are an ambassador of God.

Qualities of a good ambassador of heaven (say these in simple terms to be understood by the children)

1.      Diligent student of the Bible

2.      Prayer warriors

3.      Depending on guidance from the Holy Spirit

4.      Good behaviour

5.      Trustworthy. You must be trusted by heaven to reflect Jesus’ character

6.      Good manners

7.      Obedient

Matthew 5:16 NIV: “In the same way, let your light so shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.”

In what ways can we let Jesus’ light shine to others through us?

Here are some ideas:

·         Not biting others

·         Not hitting others

·         Obeying our parents

·         Playing nicely with our friends

·         Not being naughty

·         Not being grumpy

·         Being kind

·         Being helpful

 

 

The example of the Little Servant Girl in Naaman’s in 1 Kings Chapter 5

The story of the little maid has a great lesson for every boy and girl here today.

·         It shows that even though we are young, we are import in our neighbourhoods.

·         We should not underestimate our value in the areas where we stay or learn.

·         It helps us to open our eyes to clearly see our God-given purpose in life.

·         In good times or bad times, good or bad experiences, these may be for each of us to shine for the glory of God.

·         She might have been short, simple and in a foreign country, but she was a great witness for God. Her great influence to the people around her was tremendously felt.

·         SHE SURELY WAS A VERY GOOD AMBASSADOR FOR HEAVEN. SHE WAS A POWERFUL CHILD PREACHER!

God can also use each one of us to be powerful preachers as long as we are willing and we let Him use us as He desires. Let’s remember that we are ambassadors of heaven and we need to warn others of the terrible end of this world and those who do not follow God’s instructions.

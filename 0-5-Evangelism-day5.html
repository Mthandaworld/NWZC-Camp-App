LESSON FIVE – WATING FOR HIM

Welcome

·         Greet the children and ensure that they are well seated and quite

·         Introduce yourself and tell the children the lesson you are going to be teaching.

·         Start with the theme song of the camp, “I shall not be moved” and have a prayer.

·         Our slogan for Evangelism lessons is as follows:

-          Leader says “Evangelism”, “Evangeliving”, “I am a messenger for Jesus”, “You are a messenger for Jesus”

 

Explain to the children that:

·         Evangelism means telling others about Jesus.

·         Evangeliving means letting the way you live preach about Jesus. (e.g. the way you talk, the way you dress, what you eat, what you watch, what you listen to and the games you play.

Repeat Campmeeting 2024 Theme

-          Leader says “Campmeeting 2024”, “I shall not be moved. Faithful till the end.”. Sing the song “I shall not be moved”, with the children.

 

Know the names of the children in the class, if the class is small.

Recap of Previous Lesson, key highlights:

1.      All the superheroes on movies are not real.

2.      Jesus is the ONLY TRUE Super Hero. He is mighty and strong.

3.      I need to tell others about Jesus my only real super hero.

 

Memory Verse

 Acts 1:11 says “Men of Galilee,” they said, “why do you stand here looking into the sky? This same Jesus, who has been taken from you into heaven, will come back in the same way you have seen him go into heaven.”

 

Lesson Objectives

1.      Jesus is coming again to end all the suffering in this world and put an end to all sin.

2.      Jesus wants me to be ready for His soon return.

3.      Jesus told us things that would happen to show that His coming is soon.

4.      I need to tell others about the signs that show that Jesus is coming is soon.

5.      I need to tell others to prepare for Jesus soon return.

 

CONTENT

Has your Dad or Mum travelled for many days leaving you at home? How did you feel? Did you look forward to their coming back?

Very true, when our parents are at home, we feel safe. We become scared when they are away.

Jesus disciples were also afraid when Jesus had left them. They were not sure that they would be safe alone without Jesus.

 

Jesus had told his disciples the things that would happen, showing that His coming was very close. Let’s look at these signs of Jesus Coming. We find these in Matthew Chapter 24.

Signs of Jesus Coming

            Matthew Chapter 24 Signs

1.      There will be false prophets (Verse 4-5): We see that there are so many churches everywhere. Many do not follow what is written in the Bible. What are some of the churches that you know?

2.      There will be wars in many places (Verse 6): There is war in Syria, Israel, Palestine and many other places in the world.

3.      There will be famines and diseases (Verse 7): People are starving in Sudan, Ethiopia, India and many other places in the world. Rains are poor in many places including Zimbabwe.

4.      There will be earthquakes (Verse 7): Many earthquakes have been recorded and reported in the world, many causing a lot of damage to property and lives.

5.      There will be persecution when people are tortured and killed for following Jesus (Verse 9): This is happening in Arab countries like Saudi Arabia where Christianity was banned.

6.      People will hate each other (Verse 10): This is now everywhere as people are always fighting and doing bad things to each other.

7.      Lawlessness will increase seriously (Verse 12): Crime is on the increase, prisons are full of criminals. Robberies and plenty these days.

8.      The love of many will grow cold (Verse 12): People do not like each other anymore. They are happy hurting each other.

We find more signs in 1 Timothy Chapter 3, as follows:

9.      Men will be lovers of themselves (Verse 2)

10.  Men will be lovers of money (Verse 2)

11.  Men will be boasters (Verse 2)

12.  Men will be proud (Verse 2)

13.  Men will be blasphemers (Verse 2)

14.  Children will be disobedient to parents (Verse 2)

15.  Many people will be unthankful (Verse 2)

16.  Many people will be unholy (Verse 2)

17.  Many people will be unloving (Verse 3)

18.  Many people will be unforgiving (Verse 3)

19.  Many people will be slanderers (Verse 3)

20.  Many people will be without self-control (Verse 3)

21.  Many people will be brutal (Verse 3)

22.  Many people will be despisers of good (Verse 3)

23.  Many people will be traitors (Verse 4)

24.  Many people will be headstrong (Verse 4)

26.  Many people will be lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God (Verse 4)

27.   Many people will be having a form of godliness but denying its power (Verse 5)

Explain in a way the children will understand.

The last important sign, when fulfilled, JESUS WILL COME AGAIN! It is found in Matthew 24:14. The spreading of the gospel to everyone in this world.

Matthew 24:14 “And this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world as a witness to all nations, and then the end will come.”

This is the command that Jesus has given us:

Matthew 28:19-20 “Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age.” Amen.

We are all waiting for Jesus to come back. As we wait,

Jesus has commanded us to GO!

I cannot rest until I have spread the message to others.

You cannot rest until you have spread the message to others.

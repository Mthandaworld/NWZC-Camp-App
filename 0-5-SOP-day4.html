STORY:

A long time ago there was a group of people that were excited because they thought Jesus was about to come.  Some even sold their fields and decided that they did not need them anymore as they were going to Heaven.  There was so much excitement because no one wanted to miss Heaven, but alas something terrible happened, “Jesus did not come!” You can imagine the disappointment.  One day when a young girl was walking home from school another girl took a stone and threw it at a girl call Ellen Gould and it hit her face, breaking her nose.  Ellen could not go to school anymore and she was always sick.

When she was 17, God called her to be His Prophetess.  Remember a Prophet delivers messages from God to the people. This was the work that God called Ellen Gould to do.  Ellen went on to marry James White and most of us know her as Ellen G White. When God called she said, “Yes Lord” just like Samuel did and because of that she wrote many books which tell us where we are and where we are going. We thank God for this Prophetess and pray that we too like her can allow God to use us.

ACTIVITIES:

1.         Red light, Green light

Play the game all line up at a starting point. The goal is to be the first one to reach the end point — or finish line. When the leader says “Green!” every player runs. As soon as the leader yells “red” every player has to stop…immediately.  If anyone moves, they are OUT and cannot continue the race. We even yell “yellow” to change things up for a super slow walk.

2.         Follow the Leader

How does this teach kids to obey?  It teaches them to copy whatever the leader is doing. If the leader claps, they clap.  If the leader hops, they hop!  A classic game that teaches how important it is for everyone to follow the leaders in their life!

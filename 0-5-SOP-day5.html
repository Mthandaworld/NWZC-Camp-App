Spirit Of Prophecy

By Mrs C Charumbira

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5

DAY FIVE– IT SHALL COME TO PASS

 

Welcome

·                     Greet the children and ensure that they are well seated and quite

·                     Introduce yourself and tell the children the lesson you are going to be teaching.

·                     Start with the theme song of the camp, “I shall not be moved” and have a prayer.

Repeat Campmeeting 2024 Theme

-          Leader says “Campmeeting 2024”, “I shall not be moved. Faithful till the end.”. Sing the song “I shall not be moved”, with the children.

Know the names of the children in the class, if the class is small.

Recap of Previous Lesson, key highlights:

1.                  Ellen Gould Harmon White who was she?

2.                  What happened to her on her way home from school?

3.                  How od was she when she had her first vision?

Memory Verse

Revelation 1:7 English Standard Version, “Behold, He is coming with the clouds.”

Lesson Objectives

At the end of the lesson, children should know that:

1.                  All prophecy will come to pass.

2.                  Prophets in the Bible and Ellen Gould Harmon White foretell Christ’s return.

3.                  Jesus is coming again.

STORY:

Remember yesterday we talked about Ellen G White, well this special woman wrote many many books and she is said to have had over 2000 visions.  One great book that she has written is “The Great Controversy”.  In this book she tells how sin came into the world and how it is going to end with Jesus’ second coming.  This special book has been written for us too as children and the title of the book is, “Michael Asks Why?”  This is the book of the year and we would like everyone to read it as we prepare for the Second Coming.  Let us thank God for Sister Ellen G White who accepted to be used by Jesus and share the good news.

SONGS:

1.                  Read your Bible pray every day.

2.                  O.B.E.Y.

3.                  Jesus loves me this I know.

4.                  Jesus loves the little ones like me me me.

5.                  Lift up the trumpet.

ACTIVITIES:

1. SEEKING SIGNS FOR HIS RETURN:

"HIDE" some Bible verses written on slips of paper around the room. Let the children "SEEK" for these Bible verses that tell us that Jesus is coming again. (Examples: Luke 12:40; Luke 21:27; Matt. 24:30-31; Matt. 24:44; Matt. 25:13; Matt. 16:27; John 14:1-3; Mark 8:38;I Thess. 4:16.) Discuss that Jesus himself told us that he would return again and let the children read the Bible verse written on their slip.

2. ROLE PLAY:

(for older children): The teacher, or one of the class members will run in the room and shout that JESUS IS COMING! JESUS IS COMING! Class discussion - "When Jesus comes, will we be READY for his coming and be happy and excited, or will we be NOT READY and afraid or sad?

COLOURING ACTIVITIES:

1.                  Ellen Gould Harmon White.

2.                  Bible.

3.                  Church.

COMMITMENT

I love Jesus. I will obey God’s voice always!

CONCLUSION:

Say, Sister Ellen G white accepted the call from God. May we accept the call too. Let the kids give their answers. Other possible answers would be that they should not be afraid to do right even if no one else is doing right.

Closing Prayer

Thank you, Jesus, for today. I accept the call to be a disciple. Amen!

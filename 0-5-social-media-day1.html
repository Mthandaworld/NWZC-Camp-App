Day 1

 

The Screen as a Tool.

 

Memory Verse

 

Psalm 19:14 May the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be pleasing to you, O LORD, my rock and my redeemer.

 

Objectives

1.    The children should know that social media is a tool and can be used for good or evil

2.    The children should feel the responsibility to be a steward of social media usage.

3.    The children should respond by practicing self-control in the use of technology.

Character Word: Self Control

What is self control?

How do I practice self control?


"Hey little friends! Do you know how we can use social media like WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram?

 

- We can talk to our friends who live far away!

- We can share pictures and videos with our family and friends!

- We can learn new things and watch fun videos!

- We can even hear stories about God's love!

 

But remember, social media is just a tool, like a toy. We need to use it wisely and make good choices. Just like how we need to take care of our bodies and minds, we need to take care of our online life too!

 

Let's think about happy and nice things, like what God wants us to do! (Philippians 4:8)

 

And remember, our bodies and minds are special and belong to God! (1 Corinthians 6:19-20)

 

Let's have fun online, but always be careful and make good choices!"

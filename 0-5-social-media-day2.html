Managing Social Media

By Themba Stanely Ncube

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5

The Thief of Time.

 

Memory Text

 

Ephesians 5:16(ESV) - making the best use of the time, because the days are evil.

 

Objectives

1.    The Children Should Know Stealing time is Stealing Life

2.    The Children should Feel Social Media Steals life.

3.   

Character Word: Self Control

What is self control?

How do I practice self control?


The Children should Respond making a commitment to re-gain control of their lives from social media.

 

Hey little friends! Do you know how special time is?

 

- Time is like a gift, and we need to use it wisely!

- Just like how we celebrate our birthdays, time is precious!

- When we use time, we are using our life!

- Let's make sure to use our time well, so we can be happy and wise!

 

Just like David said in Psalm 90:12, "Teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of wisdom".

 

And remember what God says in Romans 12:2, "Don't copy the behavior and customs of this world, but let God transform you into a new person by changing the way you think".

 

Social media can be fun, but we need to be careful and not use it too much!

 

Let's use our time to love God and love others!"

 

 

 

 

Do you know how social media works?

 

- It's like a big competition for your attention!

- There are many apps that want you to use them, like games and social media sites.

- They try to keep you hooked with tricks like suggestions, ads, and notifications.

- They want you to spend more time on their app, even when you should be doing other things like reading, playing, or spending time with family and friends.

- They want to make you think that everyone is doing things that might not be good for you.

- They want to steal your time, even time that belongs to God!

- They want you to lose your love for reading the Bible and learning about God's love.

 

But remember, God loves you and wants you to use your time wisely!

- Let's be careful and make good choices online!

- Let's remember to read our Bible and learn about God's love!

- Let's not let social media trick us into wasting our time!"

The Bad Mirror

 

Reflections Can Be Tricky

 

- When we look in the mirror, we see our face!

- But sometimes we might see things that make us feel sad.

- Like when we compare ourselves to others.

 

Brand Bullying

 

Toys and Things Can't Make Us Happy

 

- Sometimes we might want things because others have them.

- But we don't need things to make us happy!

- We can play with what we have and be happy!

 

Lies Behind the Scenes

 

Not Everything is Real

 

- Sometimes what we see online isn't true!

- It's like a big pretend game!

- We have to be careful and not believe everything we see.

 

The Devil's Pulpit

 

Choose What's Good

 

- Everything we see and hear can teach us something!

- We have to choose what's good and what's bad.

- Let's choose things that make us happy and good!

 

Alone in a Crowd

 

Real Friends are Important

 

- We all want friends and love!

- But we have to be careful with online friends.

- Real friends are the ones we play with and hug!

 

Fear of Missing Out

 

It's Okay to Miss Things

 

- Sometimes we might miss things, like a party or a game.

- But it's okay! We can play another time!

- Life is more than just things we do.

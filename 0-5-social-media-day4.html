The Thief of Peace.

Memory Text

 

1 Corinthians 10:23, NLT. You say, ‘I am allowed to do anything’ but not everything is good for you. You say, ‘I am allowed to do anything’—but not everything is beneficial”

 

Objectives

The Children Should Know that social media has an effect on our bodies.

The Children should Feel responsible for protecting themselves.

Character Word: Honesty

What does it mean to be honest?

How do you stay honest when others are dishonest?


The Children should Respond by staying alert on social media.

 

Cyberbullying and Its Psychological Effects

 

Be Kind Online

 

- Some people are mean online and say hurtful things.

- This can make us feel sad, scared, and alone.

- We should always be kind and respectful online.

 

Annoying Content

 

Be Careful What You See

 

- Sometimes we might see things online that are not nice.

- Like scary or violent pictures, or bad words.

- We should always tell a grown-up if we see something bad.

 

Dangerous Challenges

 

Don't Do Things That Can Hurt You

 

- Some people online might ask us to do things that can hurt us.

- Like dangerous games or harmful activities.

- We should always say no and tell a grown-up.

 

Sharing Lies

 

Be Careful What You Share

 

- Sometimes people share things that are not true.

- We should always check if something is true before we share it.

- We don't want to hurt people by sharing lies.

 

Misleading Buying and Selling

 

Be Careful What You Buy

 

- Some people online might try to trick us into buying things we don't need.

- They might use fake pictures or false promises.

- We should always ask a grown-up before buying something online.

 

Scams

 

Don't Give Money to People You Don't Know

 

- Some people online might try to trick us into giving them money.

- They might make up a sad story or promise us something big.

- We should never give money to someone we don't know.

 

Privacy Issues

 

Know What You Share Online

 

- When we share things online, people can see them.

- We should be careful what we share and with whom we share it.

- We don't want people to know our secrets!

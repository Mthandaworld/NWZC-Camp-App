Day 5

Sharing Jesus on Social Media

 

 

Memory Text

 

 Matthew 28 : 19 -20

Go ye therefore and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Fatehr, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you, and lo I will be with you always, even unto the end of the world. amen.

 

Objectives

The Children Should Know the different ways of sharing Jesus

The Children should Feel a responsibility to share Jesus

The Children should Respond using all the ways possible to share the love of Jesus

Digital Ministry

 

Sharing God's Love Online

 

- We can share God's love with people all over the world using the internet!

- We can reach people who don't have churches near them.

- We can share hope and love with people who need it.

 

What We Do

 

- We make videos and posts to inspire people.

- We share devotionals, testimonies, and Bible stories.

- We interview people who have amazing faith stories.

- We even have games and quizzes to help people learn about God!

 

How We Do It

 

- We engage with people online and answer their questions.

- We empower people to practice what they learn.

- We lead people to churches near them.

- We work together with all the departments in the church.

 

Digital Tools

 

- We use apps like Zoom to meet online.

- We use texting apps like WhatsApp to communicate.

- We use projectors and big screens to show videos and pictures.

- We even have digital breakout rooms for smaller groups!

 

Bible Games

 

- We have games that help us learn about the Bible!

- We can challenge our friends and family to answer questions.

- We can even use power-ups to beat our best time!

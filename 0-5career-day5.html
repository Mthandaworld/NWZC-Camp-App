Day Five: The Little Maid

 

Welcome

·         Greet the children and ensure that they are well seated and quite

·         Introduce yourself and tell the children the lesson you are going to be teaching.

·         Start with the theme song of the camp, “I shall not be moved” and have a prayer.

·         If you have a catchy slogan, introduce it and repeat it with the children, one such slogan is the theme itself, “Faithful Till the end-I shall not be moved!”

·         If the classes are small, allow your children to introduce themselves. If they are big, ensure that each child who leads in any activity whether singing, bible reading or raises hand to contribute, introduces themselves.

Lesson Objective: At the end of the lesson children should learn they should care for the sick by praying for them and showing them Jesus.

Greeting & Prayer Songs:

·         Say Hello

·         I shall not be moved

·         This little light of mine

·         Read your Bible pray everyday

·         When it’s time to pray

 

Into the Lesson

Naaman was an army commander in the Syrian Army. He had led the Syrians against so many armies and had defeated them. They had also fought with the Israelites and overcame them. While raiding the Israelites, Naaman had taken a little girl who was now working as a maid in his house.

One day, he woke up not feeling good. He started scratching and scratching… soon he started having sores that’s when he realised that he had leprosy.

 

Sing: Every single cell in my body, Jump little baby

Naaman was not feeling well, every single cell in his body wasn’t happy, every single cell in his body was hurting! The little maid heard that her master wasn’t feeling well, she talked to her mistress about the prophet in Israel. She told her mistress that the prophet of Israel was able to heal her master. Sing; He is Able

Recite & Repeat the Memory Verse

Then the King shall say, “Whatever you did for others, you did it for Me.”

(Matthew 25:45 paraphrased)

Activity;

Colour in the picture of Namaan being healed in Israel

Sing: His name was Naaman

Naaman went to Israel where he was told by Elisha to deep himself in the river Jordan seven times. Naaman deeped and deeped, deeped and deeped, deeped and deeped and deeped then when he got out of the water, he was healed!

·         When others are not feeling well, we must pray for them to God who is the great physician and he will heal them.

·         Let the children take some time to pray for people who are not feeling well.

Commitment: I will pray for others who are not feeling well..

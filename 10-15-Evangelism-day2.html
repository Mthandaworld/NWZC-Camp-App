Lesson Objectives

At the end of the lesson, children should know that:

1.      God loves us very much even though we disobey many times. God wants us to be obedient and also help other to obey Him. We need to tell others to OBEY GOD and to STOP SINNING!

TODAY’S REQUIREMENTS

Today’s lesson will be taught using Road Signs. These will show us why we need to tell others about Jesus.

Start by asking what road signs the children know. Give them time to mention the road signs that they know as well as what they mean.

You will need to make very large signs for use during this lesson. You can use manilla sheet and do the signs professionally so that the children can appreciate the essence of the lesson.

These are the signs that you will need for the lesson.

CONTENT

SIGN 1- STOP SIGN

 

What does this sign mean to motorists and pedestrians?

What do motorists and pedestrians do when they approach this sign?

Why is it important to obey this sign?

 

We are all sinners, doing things that God wants us to stop doing.

·         1 Corinthians 15:34: "Sober up as you ought, and stop sinning; for some of you are ignorant of God. I say this to your shame."

·         John 5:14: "Stop sinning or something worse may happen to you."

·         1 John 3:9 New Living Translation 9 Those who have been born into God’s family do not make a practice of sinning, because God’s life is in them. So, they can’t keep on sinning, because they are children of God.

 

Use the example of Nineveh. The people were very wicked and they did terrible things that God did not like. God sent Jonah to tell the people to STOP SINNING!. When Jonah told them to stop sinning, the people of Nineveh stopped sinning, everyone of them from the youngest to the oldest.

In Jonah 3:5 (New Living Translation), it says: “The people of Nineveh believed God’s message, and from the greatest to the least, they declared a fast and put on burlap to show their sorrow.” When Jonah delivered the warning that Nineveh would be destroyed in forty days, the entire city responded with repentance and humility, even the king.

It’s a powerful example of how genuine repentance can lead to a positively changed life.

 

What Happens If We Continue Sinning?

Proverbs 29:1 NKJV: He who is often rebuked, and hardens his neck, Will suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy

Romans 6:23 New Living Translation “For the wages of sin is death.

Sin will lead us to eternal destruction!

All of us are the Jonahs of today. Like Jonah, we need to tell others that:

1.      Jesus loves each of us very much

2.      Jesus is very sad when we disobey and we do what we know is wrong

3.      Jesus wants us to stop sinning. He will help us if we are willing.

 

Questions?

1.      Who in your life can you tell this important message?

Let the children list the people they can tell. (Friends, relatives, neighbours, teachers, classmates, etc.)

 

2.      What are some ways we can use to be like Jonah and tell others to stop sinning?

The way we talk; the way we play with others; the way we eat; the respect we show for others and adults. The way we live.

 

SIGN 2- ONE WAY SIGN

What does this sign mean to motorists and pedestrians?

What do motorists and pedestrians do when they approach this sign?

Why is it important to obey this sign?

 

 

 

John 14:6 New Living Translation. Jesus told him, “I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one can come to the Father except through me.”

There is only one way to solve our sin problem, and that’s by trusting in Jesus. Trusting in anything else will not help us at all.

Instead of trusting only in Jesus, what other things can people trust in?

Allow the class to talk things that do not save us, that can be trusted in.

Afterward you can add what has not been mentioned: Wealth, money, education, friends, ancestors, livestock (goats, cows), houses, land, intelligence, strength, etc.

Each of us needs to tell others that Jesus is the only way to salvation.

 

SIGN 3- YIELD SIGN

What does this sign mean to motorists and pedestrians?

What do motorists and pedestrians do when they approach this sign?

Why is it important to obey this sign?

1 John 1:9 says “If we confess our sins, He is faithful to forgive us our sins and cleanse us from all unrighteousness”.

 

You must yield to Jesus only. Ask Him to forgive your sins and accept Him as your only Savior and Lord. Each of us needs to help others repent and yield to Jesus!

 

SIGN 4- DO NOT ENTER SIGN

 

What does this sign mean to motorists and pedestrians?

What do motorists and pedestrians do when they approach this sign?

Why is it important to obey this sign?

What do you think heaven is like? Allow the children to talk of what they have heard and what they think.

Read this verse:  1 Corinthians 2:9-10 New Living Translation “No eye has seen, no ear has heard, and no mind has imagined the things God has prepared for those who love him.”

Heaven is a wonderful place. Everything about heaven is beyond what we can think, imagine or describe. It is definitely more than lovely. Who does not want to be in heaven? None of us for sure. That is good. We should all want to be in heaven.

However, cannot enter heaven if we choose to continue sinning and doing what is wrong. We will be shown the Do Not Enter Sign and we will be forbidden to enter heaven if we do not follow God’s instructions.

Each of us needs to tell others to follow God’s instructions so that they will not be forbidden to enter heaven.

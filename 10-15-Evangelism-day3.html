2 Corinthians 5:20 New King James Version “Now then, we are ambassadors for Christ, as though God were pleading through us” 

What is an ambassador? Allow the children to describe their understanding of the word ambassador.

Ambassador:

An ambassador is an important official selected to represent his/her country in another country. They represent their country in all matters reflecting their countries mannerisms, dressing, language, culture, traditions, food and many more.

We represent heaven here on earth. Wherever you stay, or learn, you are an ambassador of God.

Qualities of a good ambassador of heaven

1.      Diligent student of the Bible

2.      Prayer warriors

3.      Depending on guidance from the Holy Spirit

4.      Good behaviour

5.      Trustworthy. You must be trusted by heaven to reflect Jesus’ character

6.      Good manners

7.      Obedient

CHILD PREACHERS

Long, long ago, in 1839, in a city called Geneva the capital city of a country called Switzerland, Pastor Louis Gaussen realised that adults did not listen to him when he was preaching about the second coming of Jesus. He believed that the second coming of Jesus was very near. He prayed to God to show him how to make the adults listen to the message.

Can anyone guess how God helped Pastor Gaussen to preach to the adults? Allow the children to give their suggestions.

God told Pastor Gaussen to train children to preach the message of the second coming. Pastor Gaussen trained the children until they new the message thoroughly and were confident to stand in streets and preach.

In 1839, Pastor Gaussen organised for the children to preach along busy streets. Many adults were attracted to listen to the children preaching. These children were powerful preachers such that hundreds and hundreds of adults accepted the message of the second coming of Jesus from the children.

God can also use each one of us to be powerful preachers as long as we are willing and we let Him use us as He desires. Let’s remember that we are ambassadors of heaven and we need to warn others of the terrible end of this world and those who do not follow God’s instructions.

 

Question?

1.      What would have happened if Pastor Gaussen had given up when the adults in Geneva did not listen to his message about the second coming of Jesus?

2.      How would you describe the willingness of the children to spread the message of the second coming of Jesus?

3.      Do you thing the children were afraid to speak in front of adults?

4.      What do you think gave the children confidence and ability to preach so powerfully leading to adults accepting the truth about the second coming of Jesus?

5.      Would you also want to be trained to preach about the second coming of Jesus?

6.      Would you be willing to speak and preach the message of the second coming of Jesus in busy places full of adults?

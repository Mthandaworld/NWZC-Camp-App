Objectives

2.    The Children should Feel Social Media Steals life.

Character Word: Self Control

What is self control?

How do I practice self control?

Stealing time is stealing life. Life is measured in time. We celebrate our birthdays using time. The more time moves, the more life moves. When you are using time, you are using your Life span. Our time on earth is limited because of sin.  When ever you use time, you are using Life.

 

Some may need to give themselves an internet/social media break until they get their priorities straightened out. David in Psalm 90:12 wisely writes: “Teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of wisdom” (NIV). Many young fall prey to the devil’s trap when it comes to the choices they make online, but God says: “Don’t copy the behavior and customs of this world, but let God transform you into a new person by changing the way you think. Then you will learn to know God’s will for you, which is good and pleasing and perfect” (Romans 12:2 NLT).

 

Social Media Inventors had a brilliant idea to change the world and make it better. The Social media creators and administrators have changed. The creators have made it a point that they hide the truth about the changes that they have made. While social media may offer some benefits, research has shown that regular use of social media causes mental health problems, addictions, lower attention spans, physical health problems, poor sleep quality, relationship problems and more. However, the most harmful effect of social media is on our hearts and our relationship with God.

 

The Invisible Secrets of Social Media

 

 The Goal is to win the Competition of social media

There are too many social media sites that have been developed. There are other products like games and productivity Apps that have been developed. A person can only use one App at a time. All these Apps are competition for your attention. This has got so bad that most Social Media Sites now have hired professionals called Attention Engineers.  The Job of the Attention Engineers is to TRAP YOU and keep you in one App.

 

Social Media Elements that Trap you in the App

·         Suggestions – You may also like this, you may also know….

·         Advertising things you were talking about on the phone or messages.

·         Connection with Fake Friends, while you ignore real friends

·         Breaking News effect – the use of the word. – Latest…New…Update

·         Live Videos – most live videos do not add value.

·         Push Notification – these are notifications that come even when the app is closed.

·         Comment section – the comment section is there to keep you hooked.

·         Get rich Quick. – there is advertising of getting easy money.

·         Counters - View Counts, Like Counts, Share Counts ( number of likes,shares)

·         Keeping up with celebrities/ Influencers – everyday they release content.

·         Infinite Scrolling there is no end to scrolling down.

·         Use of Notification sounds that you can even hear when you are sleeping.

·         Auto Play to the next video – The App would want to you play continuous videos.

·         Use of Full screen – Most of the content is made for you to use the fullscreen.

·         Use of Bright and Warm Colors

 

The Goal is to promote addiction

The new goal of social media is to make you addicted to the App, this is done by deliberate traps to keep you in the App. The addiction tactics are those used by those  who advertise gambling.

 

The Goal is to disturb you

The goal of every app is to disturb you as much as possible. When you are disturbed, it takes 23 Minutes to re-focus on what you were doing. Each App has created a lot of methods to keep you disturbed. The more you are disturbed from whatever you want to do the more time you spend time in their app.

 

 

 

The Goal is to manipulate you

The goal of every app is so that you lose self-control. Every color, button, structure has been designed to make you lose control of your behavior.

 

The Goal is to make money

All social media sites are free. You do not need money to join the App. The reason why social media sites are free is because You are the product.  Social media’s power is based on having you use the App so they can sell your information. They sell information for example Your Likes, the places you have been, your personal messages, things you are searching to buy. They sell all this information to Advertisers.

 

The Goal is to advertise the Soft Life.

TV and social media are always advertising a soft life. A soft life is an artificial life that is fulfilled by money. It is artificial because they do not truly tell you what they do behind the scenes, who sponsors them to advertise a soft life.  The truth is Life is Hard, and its Hard to everyone. Life is never fulfilled by money. Money is very important and can make life easy, but it can never solve that Life is Hard for everyone.  Money and soft life cannot solve mental health issues, It cannot solve that some days are just bad, it can solve death and trauma, it cannot solve bad attitudes and behavior, it cannot solve cruelty and pain. It cannot solve Heart breaks and depression. Our only hope and solution to life is found in Jesus Christ as our savior.

 

The Goal is to sell and advertise Sin

All the things that are advertised as against God are advertised as being very normal. The Sin is repeated until your mind believes that is very normal. They advertise sin until you think that everyone is doing it. Most of the times, they are laughing at the bible stories and say the bible is lying.

 

The Goal is to steal your Time

The trap that is set is so that you spend as much time as possible. When you are supposed to be reading your school books, you can spend your time watching Tik Tok videos. Instead of reading your bible, you can spend time. Instead of cooking or doing chores you could spend your time on social media. Instead of Working or school you could use the wifi to watch other things. Most importantly it steals your time from God. Instead of listening In church, you steal God’s time and use your phone in church. Instead of coming to church in the afternoon you can spend the afternoon on the Phone, stealing God’s time. Breaking the Sabbath Commandment.

 

The Goal is to make you lose your Love for the Bible.

Satan’s goal is to break the attention of those who read the bible to have a short concentration. Reading the bible takes time and concentration. Most of the times when a phone addicts read the bible, they sleep because they lose their ability to concentrate for a long time. The bible teaches serious principles and values. Social media says principles are not important, you can do what ever you want as long as it makes you happy. The bible says you cannot do whatever you want, you have to follow God’s way of life.

How to Keep Safe from Social Media Trap

·         Read the bible regularly.

·         do not Share things that are against God.

·         Be busy, Look for productive things to do

·         Switch off Push Notifications and Lock screen Notifications.

·         Unfollow influencers and celebrities

·         Unfollow Gossip Pages

·         Social Media Fasting

·         Be satisfied and content with what you have, do not envy what you can never have.

·         Do not concentrate on Likes, reactions, comments and views.

THE BAD MIRROR.

 

The purpose of the mirror is to reflect your image. There are two types of people that go to the mirror. The first person goes to the mirror to check if everything is in its rightful place, the other person goes to the mirror to compare what they look like with other people. The first person usually comes from the mirror satisfied and confident to face the world. The second person who goes to mirror to compare themselves with other people, moves from the mirror with doubt, anxious and sad. Comparison is the thief of joy.

The digital screens via social media, Movies, series, adverts are a Bad mirror of life. We usually look ourselves in comparison of the people. The self-image is lost. Most of the times someone will take a lot of pictures with different angles, only to choose one they think is the best. The truth is all those pictures you think are ugly are all you. The truth is you do not walk around in that perfect angle, with perfect light and perfect background you took the picture in.


The social media Mirror is worse because you share your image with the hope that people will like your pictures or react to your picture. Social media amplifies the negative thoughts as you compare with others. When people do not react to your picture, usually you lose your joy. Sometimes you remove the picture or video. The truth is likes and reactions do not change how you look, the comments made do not make you better.

 

BRAND BULLYING

This is pressure that we get to buy a particular product or item because “everyone has one”. Sometimes some buy a particular product so that they can show others. This is a kind of peer pressure that is very common at school and church. Sometimes people are pressured to buy things they cannot afford so that they can fit in. People who cannot afford the fashionable items are isolated or bullied. Most popular shows and content of social media make brand bullying very normal. They encourage a life of brands as a way of life. This crushes the confidence of most children and adults.

 

LIES BEHIND THE SCENES

Not everything we see online is real. Most of the things we watch is fake or fantasy. Most of the interesting and captivating content online is not real. This kind of entertainment is dangerous because sometimes it can convivence us that its real. Some of the situations are relatable, similar to real life, but it does not make it real. In some cases it encourages that you create fake versions of yourself using filters and Artificial technology.

 

Most people online have borrowed this strategy. Social media only shows and promotes highlights of their lives through short stories and status updates. Most of these stories show only a part of the story. People want to show the good side of life, it makes those who look at these stories or status feel like their life is not interesting. In life there is always something better, no matter what you do, there is always be someone better. Be content with what you have. Most of the social media influencers are sponsored by certain companies or people to post the life they are Posting. Sometimes its like we are comparing apples and oranges. We never know how people get their money, we never know how people get where they go, we never know what values they compromised to get the picture that they will be taking. In most cases there are a lot of people who fake to live a life they display.

 

 

THE DEVIL’S PULPIT

Everything that happens on earth is a sermon. Its either it’s a sermon that represents God or it’s a sermon that represents the devil. The strategy of Satan has always been mixing truth with error. A 100% lie is 99% truth and 1% error.  Satan has planted error in every content that is made. Every video, Every movie, every song, every series has a sermon message of error. The message is put as a joke or as a passive statement. Any word that promotes sin, is a message that acts as a sermon for the devil. Sometimes we spend countless hours watching a person committing a sin, they perfect the way they sin. Some songs describe how to sin and enjoy Sinning against God. All this is teaching you to disobey God. Anything that does not give glory to God, is preaching for the Devil.  Nothing is innocent, the devil hides his messages even on things that seem innocent.

 

ALONE IN A CROWD.

 

We all want to be loved, given attention, belong and feel worth. The social media creators know this, they trick you to feel like you can get this from their own site/ App. The trick is to use attention. They suggest a number of friends or connections that you may know. When you add the friend you form a superficial relationship. You feel like you are connected to their lives. It’s the same effect that you get when you follow a celebrity, you secretly feel like you know them, you know what they like eating, what their favorite colors are, who their other friends are. In most cases you may start not paying attention to the people that are around you. May pay less attention to the friends you already have. May also pay less attention to family when you create your own digital family of friends.

 

 

FEAR OF MISSING OUT

We want to belong. We want to belong to a group of people. We already belong to a family, to a church, to a school, to a group of friends. The social media provides a new sense of belonging. It keeps updating events and programs. The programs are usually entertaining, as they happen a sense of I am missing out is created. When you do not get to participate you fear that you have missed out. Missing out creates a false sense that I do not belong. When you meet other people who were at the event, you may feel like you do are out of place. Life is not made of events, its okay to miss an event, its okay not to know what is going on. Life continues weather you know or you do not know.

 

Commitment.

I commit myself be content with who I am and what I have. God is loving he will always provide all my needs according to his will.

How can I serve the Online Community?

Digital Ministry

 

Do you know how we can tell others about Jesus and his love, even if they're far, far away? We can use the internet and social media to share the Good News with people all around the world!

 

Just like how missionaries travel to different places to share the Gospel, we can use digital tools to reach people in places that are hard to get to. And the best part? People can access the Gospel anytime, anywhere, in their own language!

 

We can be a part of this amazing work by sharing Jesus' love online, praying for others, and being kind to everyone we meet in person and online. Let's be digital disciples and help spread God's love to the whole world!"

 

 

 

Focus of Digital Ministry Online.

Do you know how we can use social media to help others and share God's love? It's not just about sharing church services or events, but about reaching people who don't know Jesus yet.

 

We want to inspire hope in a world that can sometimes seem hopeless. And we can do that by being kind, helping others, and sharing God's love online.

 

Churches can use social media to connect with their community in a meaningful way, like a friend who cares. It's not just about posting information, but about talking to people, listening to them, and helping them.

 

Every church is unique and special, and we can all use our talents and resources to serve others and help them know God. We want to be like a letter that everyone reads, a letter of love and hope.

 

Let's use social media to build relationships and share God's goodness with the world!"

 

There are different programs that we could create and share online.

Do you know how we can share God's love and inspiring stories online? We can create different programs like:

 

- Devotionals: Imagine someone reading a special message from a book, or recording a short video about how God helped them recently. It's like a daily dose of encouragement!

 

- Testimonies: People sharing their amazing stories of how God helped them through tough times, like a superhero saving the day! It's so inspiring to hear how God works in people's lives.

 

- Children's videos: Fun songs, Bible stories, puppet shows, and more! Like a special kids' church service online.

 

- Interviews: Imagine talking to people who have amazing stories of faith, like a church member or pastor. We can learn so much from their experiences and advice.

 

- Biblical Study: Learning and memorizing Bible verses, and studying our Sabbath school lessons together. It's like a virtual Bible club!

 

We can share all these programs online, like a special delivery of love and inspiration! Let's share God's love with the world, and inspire others to love Him too!"

 

 

 

Steps to a successful Digital Ministry

Do you know how we can share God's love online? Here are some steps to make our digital ministry successful:

 

WE ENGAGE - We ask people to share their stories and questions in the comments, and our online Chaplain helps answer them and provides helpful resources.

 

WE EMPOWER - We encourage people to practice what we share, like being kind and helping others. We share testimonies of God's goodness and love.

 

WE LEAD - Our online ministry is like a bridge to our physical church, where we can meet friends and learn more about God. It leads us to baptism and a closer relationship with God.

 

WE COLLABORATE - Just like how our church has different teams working together, we work with all departments to create content that helps us grow in faith.

 

Let's work together to share God's love online and offline!"

Digital Tools that can be used at church.

 

Do you know how we can use technology to learn about God and connect with our church friends?

 

- Virtual Platforms: We can have online classes and meetings using apps like Zoom, just like how we have class in a physical classroom!

 

- Texting: We can use apps like WhatsApp and Facebook to communicate with our church friends and group, even scheduling texts ahead of time.

 

- Projectors and Big Screens: We can use visuals like pictures and videos to help us understand Bible lessons and sermons better.

 

- Interactive Polls and Quizzes: We can answer questions and give feedback during lessons and sermons, making it more fun and engaging!

 

- Digital Breakout Rooms: We can break into smaller groups online, just like how we do in physical church, and discuss topics together.

 

- Bible Based Games: We can play games that help us learn more about the Bible and its heroes, and even challenge our friends and family!

 

Let's use technology to grow in faith and have fun while doing it!"

 

Commitment.

I commit to utilize my social media in sharing Jesus in all I do and say things that will bring people to have a relationship with him.

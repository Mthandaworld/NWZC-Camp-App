SESSION 1: INTRODUCTION

 

This Camp Meeting lesson is not meant to prove that spiritualism is unbiblical and thus hated by God. It is not to excite people with things that happen around the world of spiritualism. We know these things too well. We have to be careful and avoid giving Satan and his woks much undeserved attention. This lesson has the following objectives:

·         To alert the people of the snares and dangers of spiritualism

·         To reveal the willingness and power of Christ to deliver those ensnared in spiritualism

·         To empower members to be able to communicate God’s truth with love

·         To avoid extreme positions by those who know the truth

 

What is Spiritualism?

 

Spiritualism is a belief that the dead can interact with the living, either directly or through the agency of a medium. The idea that the dead can communicate presupposes that the dead are conscious and therefore able to see, hear, speak, move in a more exalted sphere than the one they existed in before death. These ‘spirits’ believed to be of the dead people are actually evil angels or demons headed by Satan himself.

 

Biblical record

The bible gives us the history of the great controversy between Satan and God.

A highly ranked cherub or angel among God’s angels who was Lucifer began to be proud and envious of God. Despite having been created perfect, this anointed cherub, iniquity was later found in him (Ezekiel 28:14), because he had desired to be worshipped (Isaiah 14:12-14). There was war in heaven, as Christ with His angels fought against the rebellious angel who had become Satan (Revelation 12:7-9), with a third (Revelation 12:4) of all heaven’s angels who had joined him. Ultimately, Satan and his angel followers were cast out of heaven and there was no more place for them in heaven. They are here on earth, where they came filled with great wrath for they know they have a short time.

 

The ministry of fallen angels is mistaken for the ministry of holy angels, because the evil angels disguise themselves to appear as angels of light (2 Cor 11:14). Evil angels also disguise themselves and interact with people as their dead friends and relatives or even enemies. “The doctrine of man’s consciousness in death, especially the belief that spirits of the dead return to minister to the living, has prepared a way for modern spiritualism.” E.G. White, Great Controversy, p. 551.

 

According to the bible, the doctrine of man’s consciousness in death was introduced to man in the temptation to eat of the forbidden tree, (Genesis 3:1-4). It was one of the lies used by Satan to convince man that disobeying God was not as fatal as they had heard from God.

‘Then the serpent said to the woman, “You will not surely die.”’ (Genesis 3:4). This was the first sermon ever preached upon the immortality of the soul. The divine sentence, “Dust you are, and to dust you shall return,” points to utter extinction of life. After the fall, Satan bade his angels make a special effort to inculcate the belief in man’s natural immortality…” E. G. White, Great Controversy, p. 534.

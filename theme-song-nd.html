144 Emhlabeni Olidundu

English Shona Ndebele

Ngi qond’ indlel’ enyukayo Ngi khwela zonk’ izinsuku. Ngi nguye okhulekayo; Nkosi, ngi mise phezulu.

CHORUS

Nkosi, ngi simamiseke Emhlabeni olidundu, Endaweni ephakheme Nkosi, ngi mise phezulu.

A ngi thandi ukuhlala Lapho kunenkan’ enkulu. Kumb’ abanye, ba ngahlala, Mina ngi thanda phezulu.

Ngiph’ inhlalw’ ehlanzekile uSathan’ u yangigwaza; Kodw’ inkolo yam’ I zwile; Nkosi, Ngi mise phezulu.

Ngi zokhwel’ esiqongweni Ngi bone ukukhazimla; Ngi finyel’ ezulwini Nkosi, ngi mise phezulu.
